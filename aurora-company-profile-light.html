Who We Are

Our Story

Aurora Solutions was founded in Addis Ababa by Naol Tamrat, a Software Engineering graduate of AASTU and team lead at Africa to Silicon Valley (A2SV), and Natan, an entrepreneur with deep roots in Ethiopian business development.


We started in 2023 with a simple observation: Ethiopian and African businesses were invisible online, with no websites, no branding, and no digital infrastructure, and that invisibility was limiting their reach. We began solving that problem for local clients and quickly expanded to serve organizations in Kenya, Germany, Canada, and the USA.


Today, Aurora Solutions operates as a full-service digital agency with a team built to deliver at global standards from right here in Addis Ababa.

Naol Tamrat — Co-Founder

BSc in Software Engineering from AASTU. Team Lead at A2SV (Africa to Silicon Valley). Specializes in full-stack development, mobile applications, and engineering leadership. Brings a portfolio of web and mobile applications built to international standards.

Natan — Co-Founder

Self-built entrepreneur with expertise in creative strategy, client relations, and team leadership. Drives Aurora's vision, business development, and operations. Has worked with diverse clients across Ethiopia and internationally.
